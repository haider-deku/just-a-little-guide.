Definition

Anime is a type of animation that appeared and became famous in Japan, and is characterized by its bright graphics and colors that express vibrant characters.
The anime has used many of the characteristics that are unique and that distinguish Japan from others.
The big eyes that are used in anime characters are a Japanese symbol and reference that refers to the term (windows to the soul).
It is worth noting that anime is not only for children, but there are also anime films that deal with adult topics as well.
The word anime is an abbreviation of the term animation (in English: animation), which is a word used within Japan to refer to all Japanese animation,
and outside Japan the word anime is used to refer to Japanese animation only. The anime has become a local Japanese product,
the story is the most prominent of its elements, and it is worth noting that anime has become an international phenomenon that attracts millions of viewers,
iand anime films have been translated into many international languages, and the anime is not limited to a specific genre but is comprehensive,
as it appears in books and films, as well as It embodies comedy, adventure, science fiction, horror, and other types of animation.
