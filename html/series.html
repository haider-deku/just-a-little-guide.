Series guid

Definition

Series is a series of consecutive drama episodes broadcast on television or on some applications or websites.
Most of the time, it is divided into episodes and each episode is part of the series.
Each episode of the series introduces us to certain events and then stops at a certain point, and the events are completed in the following episode.

Type of series:

  • Comedy series
  • Dramatic series
  • Horror series
  • Historical series
